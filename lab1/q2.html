Dinner Chicken Karahi
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt and red chili powder to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  1. Heat oil in a karahi or wok, add cumin seeds.
  2. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomatoes, and cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add chicken pieces, yogurt, coriander powder, salt, and red chili powder. Cook until chicken is done.
  5. Garnish with chopped green chilies and fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot.
Dinner Namkeen Gosht
  • 1 kg mutton, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  1. Heat oil in a large pan, add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add mutton pieces, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the meat is tender.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with naan or rice.
